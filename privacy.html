Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026

This privacy policy describes how TobyAI Pty Ltd ("Toby", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information. We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

What we collect

When you register your interest through tobyai.io, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your business name
  • Information about your business, including annual revenue, industry, and region
  • Your role (business owner, CFO, accounting firm partner, investor or advisor)
  • The date and time you submitted the form

We do not collect financial account information, tax file numbers, banking details, or sensitive information through the registration form.

How we use it

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you about your registration of interest
  • Assess fit for the Toby founding cohort or accounting firm partner programme
  • Provide quotes and product information
  • Improve our product and offering based on aggregated, de-identified insights about who is interested in Toby

We do not sell your information to third parties. We do not use your information for marketing unrelated to Toby.

Where it is stored

Information collected through tobyai.io is stored in Google Workspace (Google Sheets and Gmail) and our internal customer relationship management tooling. These services are operated by Google LLC and may store data outside Australia.

When you become a Toby customer, your financial and operational data is stored in Microsoft Azure's Australia East region with disaster recovery in Australia Southeast. Data residency for customer data is non-negotiable: your financial data does not leave Australia.

Who we share it with

We share information only where necessary to operate our business:

  • With service providers who help us run our business (email, CRM, analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations
  • If required by law, court order, or regulatory request
  • Where you have given us specific consent to share, for example with an accounting firm partner you have asked us to engage with

How long we keep it

We retain information you provide for as long as necessary to provide the service you have requested, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you no longer wish to be contacted, we will delete or de-identify your information on request, subject to any retention obligations under Australian law.

Your rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request that we stop using your information for marketing
  • Make a complaint about how we have handled your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tobyai.io.

Complaints

If you believe we have breached the Australian Privacy Principles, please contact us first at hello@tobyai.io. We will investigate and respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you can lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

Customer financial data and pattern analysis

This section applies once you become a Toby customer. It does not apply to information you submit through the registration of interest form.

When you onboard onto Toby, you give us access to your financial data: your ledger, your bank feeds, your invoices, your payroll, your project costs. That data is yours. We hold it on your behalf in the Australia East region. We do not sell it. We do not give it to other Toby customers. We do not use your raw financial data to train AI models.

Patterns extracted from across the Toby customer base, for example how a particular type of expense is typically categorised in a particular industry, are anonymised and aggregated before they leave your tenant. These patterns make the system smarter for every customer. They cannot be used to re-identify your business, your transactions, or your people.

Customers who join the founding cohort with three or more years of historical Xero data make the system materially better at understanding Australian SMBs, and their migration and implementation fees are waived in recognition of this. Their data is treated under exactly the same protections as every other customer's.

AI providers and your data

Toby uses AI services from Anthropic, Microsoft Azure AI Foundry, and other providers under our LiteLLM proxy. We never send the following to AI providers under any circumstances:

  • Tax file numbers
  • Bank account numbers
  • Salary or payroll figures
  • Tenant identifiers that could re-identify your business

Patterns extracted at the AI layer are anonymised and aggregated. Every AI call our product makes is audited. AI providers under our agreements are bound by zero-data-retention or equivalent terms where relevant.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated to people whose information we hold by email.
