Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026

1. Who we are

Toby AI ("we", "us", "our") is an Australian company based in Brisbane, Queensland. We build AI-powered financial software for Australian small and medium businesses. This policy explains how we handle personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. What we collect

When you register your interest via our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Business name
  • Work email address
  • Annual revenue range
  • Industry

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you when your early access tier opens
  • Send relevant product updates about Toby AI
  • Understand the types of businesses interested in our product

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. How we store your information

Registration data is stored in Google Sheets via Google Apps Script. Data transmitted to and from our website is encrypted using TLS. When our product launches, all customer data will be hosted in Australia on Microsoft Azure.

5. Third-party services

Our website currently uses:

  • GitHub Pages — to host the website
  • Google Apps Script — to process registration form submissions

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data.

6. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of future communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@tobyai.io.

7. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking scripts.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or wish to make a complaint, contact us at hello@tobyai.io.
