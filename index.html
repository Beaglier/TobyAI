How It Works

Two layers. Complete confidence.

A deterministic rules engine handles every financial calculation. AI sits above it, adding intelligence without ever touching your ledger.

1

Connect Your Business

Link bank accounts, import your chart of accounts, and set up your team with role-based access in minutes.

2

Rules Engine Handles Compliance

All financial calculations, journal entries, and ATO compliance logic run deterministically. No AI inference on your numbers.

3

AI Works in the Background

20+ specialist agents monitor, reconcile, forecast, and flag exceptions. They never write directly to your ledger.

4

You Stay in Control

Review AI recommendations, approve exceptions, and ask questions in plain English. Toby surfaces insight, you make the call.
