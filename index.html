How it works

Two layers. Complete confidence.

A deterministic ledger and rules engine handle every financial calculation. AI sits above it, adding intelligence without ever touching your numbers. As more Australian SMBs join, the system learns the patterns of how AU businesses actually run. Your numbers stay yours. The patterns make the system smarter for everyone.

Layer 1, deterministic

The ledger and rules engine

Every journal entry, every BAS calculation, every payroll run, every depreciation schedule. Deterministic, reproducible, auditable. No AI inference touches your numbers.

Statutory rates and thresholds, including PAYG withholding, SG, payroll tax by state, ATO effective life, and Award rates, are pulled directly from the ATO, Fair Work, and state revenue offices. The CA-led compliance team owns the interpretation work where judgement is required: BAS labels, GST treatments, FBT, TPAR, AASB application, Payday Super. All sourced, all dated, all reviewed before release.

Layer 2, AI

The intelligence layer

Twenty AI agents read the ledger, monitor exceptions, prepare drafts, propose actions, and surface insight. They never write to the ledger directly. Every AI action is logged, audited, and reversible.

All AI calls are routed through a proxy with no hardcoded model references. Raw financial identifiers, including TFNs, bank details, salary data, and tenant identifiers, never leave the Australian system boundary. Patterns extracted at the AI layer are anonymised and aggregated.
